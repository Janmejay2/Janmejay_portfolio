    About Ravenshaw University
    Ravenshaw University is a prestigious higher education institution located in Cuttack, Odisha, India. It is one of the oldest and largest universities in the state and has a rich history that dates back to 1868. The university offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs across various disciplines. Originally known as Ravenshaw College, it was established as an intermediate college affiliated with the University of Calcutta. In 1917, it was granted autonomous status and became one of the leading educational institutions in the region. In 2006, Ravenshaw College was granted university status and renamed Ravenshaw University.
